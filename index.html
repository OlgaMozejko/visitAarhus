    Aros Museum

    Aarhus C, 8000

    Aros Museum is a must-see attraction and at the same time an architecture highlight in Denmark

    It is in one of the largest museums in the Nordic countries and on the roof, you can experience an amazing Rainbow panorama tour. It is a 150 metre long circular panoramic path with 360 degrees view of the surrounding Aarhus city.
